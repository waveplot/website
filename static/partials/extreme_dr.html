Extreme Dynamic Range

This page shows the releases in the database with the best and the worst dynamic range. Dynamic range is a measure of the quality of music, and how much variation there is between the quieter parts and the noisier parts. In recent years, production has focused on applying more and more compression to music. The aim of this is to make songs sound louder in an attempt make them stand out, but it actually reduces sound quality and fidelity, and lowers the dynamic range.
